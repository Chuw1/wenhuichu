About Me

Howdy! I am an Instructional Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Texas A&M University. My research focuses primarily on Artificial intelligence, Machine Learning, Robotics, and Computer Vision. I earned my Master of Science degree in Computer Science at Boston University, and my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Houston, where I worked with Dr. Nikolaos V. Tsekos concentrated on computational methods for MRI-guided and powered ferric applicators, including modeling and image processing.

Before my appointment at Texas A&M, I served as a visiting professor at the Texas A&M University-Victoria.
